Our Collections

At LMEAN, each collection tells a unique story, inspired by the diversity and richness of cultures around the world.

Valentine's Day - L'amour Plastique

A romantic and bold series, perfect for expressing love and passion. The L'amour Plastique collection combines modern style with classic Valentine's motifs, creating pieces that are both daring and deeply meaningful.

Morocco - Desert Mosaic

Inspired by the mystical colors and textures of Moroccan deserts and cities, our Desert Mosaic collection features earthy tones and intricate patterns. The centerpiece, a brown t-shirt adorned with a vibrant mosaic, captures the essence of Moroccan artistry.

IDENTITY - The Global Tapestry

The IDENTITY collection celebrates individuality and the global community. With bold designs and a vibrant color palette, it reflects the diverse stories and backgrounds that shape our world, symbolizing unity in diversity.

Our Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to using only the finest materials in our garments. Our signature fabric is 100% organic cotton, known for its comfort, durability, and environmental sustainability. Each piece of clothing is designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that it not only looks good but feels good too.

T-Shirts

Our t-shirts are crafted from 100% organic cotton, sourced from eco-friendly farms. We ensure that each t-shirt is soft to the touch, yet durable enough to withstand daily wear and tear. The fabric is pre-washed to prevent shrinkage and maintain its shape and color over time. The designs are printed with eco-friendly inks that are gentle on the skin and the environment.

Sweatshirts

Our sweatshirts are designed for comfort and style. We use a blend of organic cotton and recycled polyester to create a fabric that is both soft and resilient. The interior is brushed for added softness, providing a cozy feel. Each sweatshirt features reinforced seams for durability and a fit that is both modern and comfortable. The attention to detail extends to the cuffs and hem, which are ribbed to retain their shape.

Quality control is integral at every stage of production. We meticulously inspect each garment for any imperfections, ensuring that our customers receive only the best. By choosing LMEAN, you are not just buying a piece of clothing; you are investing in a garment that stands the test of time, both in style and durability.

About LMEAN

At LMEAN, we believe in the power of fashion as a means of personal expression and a reflection of individual identity. Established in the vibrant heart of France and infused with a Spanish spirit, our brand represents a unique fusion of two rich cultural influences.

Our Journey

LMEAN began as a small but ambitious project by a group of Spanish designers who were mesmerized by the elegance and sophistication of French fashion. They envisioned a brand that would blend the laid-back, expressive nature of Spanish design with the timeless elegance of French couture. It was from this vision that LMEAN was born – a brand that stands for quality, affordability, and style.
